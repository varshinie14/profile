About Muthamil

Muthamil Arasu Jayakanthan is a passionate musician who brings stories to life through rhythm and melody. His music fuses deep emotion with raw energy — creating a unique sound that connects hearts and moves souls.

Whether performing live or composing in the studio, Muthamil believes in music that speaks truth. His songs are inspired by real experiences, dreams, and emotions that make listeners feel every note.
